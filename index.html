Transforming the Lives of Young Adults Through Fitness

Upower provides free fitness classes to underserved teens in their neighborhoods. These classes incorporate life skills critical to breaking the cycle of poverty.

Get involved if you own a CrossFit affiliate, run a community program for teens, or are involved in teen education and would like to help.

Learn more about Upower and the problem the team is trying to solve.

Our team is the backbone of our success. We are only able to make a positive impact by bringing on the best and the most passionate about our cause.
